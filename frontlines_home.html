Quick Answers for Curious Minds

Dark Mode FAQ Accordion
What is Frontlines Edutech and what do you offer? ▶

Frontlines Edutech is the largest Telugu-speaking EdTech community offering job-oriented training in Java Full Stack, Python, Salesforce, AWS, DevOps, Digital Marketing, UI/UX, and more. We empower freshers, career switchers, and working professionals with industry-ready skills and placement support.

Do I need a technical background to join your courses? ▶

No! Many of our learners come from non-IT backgrounds. We start from scratch and guide you step-by-step, making our training suitable for freshers, degree students, homemakers, and domain switchers.

Is the training available in Telugu or English? ▶

We teach in a mix of Telugu + English to ensure maximum clarity and comfort. This makes our classes more engaging and easy to understand, especially for students from AP & Telangana.

Will I get a certificate after completing the course? ▶

Yes. You will receive a verifiable course completion certificate from Frontlines Edutech, which you can showcase on your resume and LinkedIn profile.

Do you provide placement support or job assistance? ▶

Absolutely. We offer placement guidance, resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, and job referrals through our network of recruiters and hiring partners.

What is the duration of your courses? ▶

Our course durations range from 8 to 12 weeks, depending on the track. For example, Java Full Stack is around 90+ hours, Salesforce is 40+ hours, and AI/Data Analytics courses span 100+ hours.

Is it live training or recorded? ▶

All our courses include live interactive classes. Recordings are also provided so you can revise or learn at your own pace even after the class.

Are there EMI or payment options available? ▶

Yes. We provide flexible payment options including EMI facilities so that learning is accessible to everyone.

How can I join a demo session? ▶

We conduct regular demo sessions for each course. You can register through our website or message us on WhatsApp/Telegram to get instant access to upcoming sessions.

Why should I choose Frontlines Edutech over others? ▶

We’re not just a training company – we’re a community. With 100,000+ learners, regional support, bilingual delivery, real-time projects, and top trainer mentorship, we bridge the gap between education and employability in a truly personalized way.
